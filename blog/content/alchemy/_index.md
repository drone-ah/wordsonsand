I work with founders and executive teams when something important is stuck.

Often, the underlying issue is a decision carrying real financial, structural,
or reputational consequence - about direction, ownership, architecture, risk, or
whether to continue.

In a focused session, we bring that decision into the open, make the trade-offs

## Where It Becomes Valuable

Alchemy is useful when a decision with consequence is present but not yet
closed.

For example:

- Friction between teams is eroding trust
- Reactive work has become the default
- Progress depends on a small number of individuals
- Executive decision rights are unclear
- A scale-up, rebuild, pivot, or shutdown is under consideration

You don't need another discussion - you need a decision.

## What I Do

In a focused 90-minute session, we work from the friction you are experiencing
to the decision shaping it.

We:

- Clarify the tensions that are present
- Trace them back to gaps in alignment or mandate
- Identify the unmade decision at the centre
- Clarify who owns the final call
- Make the real trade-offs explicit
- Close it

You leave with a decision made and a clear mandate to act.
