Every single cell,\
Every single atom,\
Of my mind,\
Of my body,\
Of my soul.

My whole being loved her,\
Loved her totally,\
Loved her completely,\
Loved her unconditionally.

She took my hand,\
kissed it sweetly,\
and just walked away
