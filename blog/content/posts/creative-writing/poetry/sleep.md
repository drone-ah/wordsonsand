Ill sleep when I am dead.\
Spending all night thinking of you,\
against my will,\
against my tired body,\
my tired mind,\
and my tired heart.

Lying here, thinking of you,\
unable to sleep,\
all night long.\
all week long,\
all life long.

Until it is time to sleep.
